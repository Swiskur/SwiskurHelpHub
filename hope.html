What is the Angels of Hope Mission

Angels of Hope, an all-volunteer foundation, is committed to providing emergency financial assistance to families with children across the state of Michigan that have demonstrated they are experiencing financial hardship due to a cancer diagnosis.


About Angels of Hope

One of Angels of Hope’s key objectives is to ensure that no Michigan family battling cancer is ever denied assistance due to a lack of funding. We prioritize fundraising through 3rd party events, grants, and corporate sponsorships. We are also looking to expand our team of dedicated volunteers — particularly in our Grant Committee, Finance Committee, and Fundraising Committee. Our goal is to help ease the burden caused by the disease, its treatment and its devastating effects – both financial and emotional.
